Finance teams need clean paper trails: invoices for accruals, receipts for expense reimbursements, and tax IDs that match what is on file with your vendors. Keep addresses and VAT numbers current before month-end close to avoid delays.

## Find your invoices

Navigate to **Settings → Billing → Invoices** to access PDF copies and detailed line items for every billing period. Download CSV or structured exports if your ERP system can ingest them automatically.

### Verify line items

Check seat counts and add-ons against your internal headcount reports each period. Discrepancies often trace to deactivated users still occupying paid seats until the next billing true-up.

## Update tax and VAT details

Update your tax IDs before renewal to avoid processing delays. If you operate across multiple legal entities or countries, confirm which party remits VAT or sales tax for each entity, and store evidence with the invoice PDF for audit readiness.

<Note>
Key Nine Studios billing does not constitute tax advice. Run final numbers past your accounting partner, especially for cross-border entities or reverse-charge scenarios.
</Note>

## Download receipts

Card charges include a receipt link suitable for expense tools that accept emailed PDFs. Forward receipts immediately after a charge—matching expenses six months later is significantly more time-consuming.

### Corporate card rotations

If the cardholder on the billing profile changes, update the billing contact immediately so receipts reach the person who must attest to expenses during reconciliation.
