Key Nine Studios collects and processes personal data to operate the platform, secure your account, and improve reliability. This page summarizes what we collect, why we collect it, how long we keep it, and what rights you have regarding your data.

## Data we process

Key Nine Studios processes account data (name, email, organization), usage telemetry (feature interactions, error events), and content you store or create within the platform.

### Sensitive categories

Key Nine Studios does not intentionally collect sensitive personal data such as health, financial, or children's data. If your use case requires processing such categories, contact us before onboarding.

## Purposes

We process your data to:

- Operate and deliver the Key Nine Studios platform
- Secure accounts and detect abuse
- Improve product reliability and feature quality
- Communicate service updates and support responses

### Analytics

Key Nine Studios may use analytics and error tracking to understand product usage and diagnose issues. Where applicable, you can opt out via your account privacy settings or by contacting support.

## Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. See the [regional specifics](/support/legal/regions) articles for jurisdiction-specific details.

### Submit a request

To exercise your data rights, contact Key Nine Studios support with your request type, account email, and the data in question. We aim to respond within the timeframe required by applicable law in your jurisdiction.
