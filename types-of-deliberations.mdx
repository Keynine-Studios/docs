---
title: "Types of Deliberations"
icon: "gavel"
description: "Overview of small, medium, and big deliberation types in the Deliberate app, with time limits of 10 seconds, 10 minutes, and 10 hours for each type."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Small Deliberation">
    This is typically for decisions that can be made within a few seconds, for example, where should I go to eat. When deliberating on one of these, you get 10 seconds.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Medium Deliberation">
    This is typically for decisions that can be made within a few minutes, for example, which job offer should I take/should I go on a vacation. When deliberating on one of these, you get 10 minutes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Big Deliberation">
    This is typically for decisions that are hard to make and take multiple hours, for example, should I move/should I quit my job. When deliberating on one of these, you get 10 hours.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

After your time is up, you must make a decision, otherwise your favorite apps on your phone will be locked down!
