<Steps>
  <Step title='From the "Explore" tab'>
    1. Click on "Feed" on the bottom bar
    2. Find the post you want to react to
    3. Click on either the 🔥 or ❤️ emoji
    4. To remove your reaction, simply click on the icon again
  </Step>
  <Step title="Completed!">
    You can now add and remove reactions from posts in Top 10!
  </Step>
</Steps>
