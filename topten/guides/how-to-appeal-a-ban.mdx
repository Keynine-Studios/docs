You will know you are banned. To see this, please open the Top 10! App. It will display an account suspended screen. 

**TO APPEAL:** [Use this form!](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScXbpCLE7Azp3nfhEUJtBrxUFBZ-3b7sZUqt-8z1WtAAP9E0w/viewform?usp=dialog)

<Steps>
  <Step title="To appeal a ban">
    1. Fill out all information, including name, email, phone number, reason for ban
    2. Please write a well-crafted, thoughtful paragraph on why we should unban you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Completed!" stepNumber={2}>
    You have now completed your ban appeal
  </Step>
</Steps>
