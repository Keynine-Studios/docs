---
title: "How to Appeal a Ban"
description: "Top 10! support article"
---

You will know you are banned. To see this, please open the Top 10! App. It will display an account suspended screen. **TO APPEAL:**

[Use this form!](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScXbpCLE7Azp3nfhEUJtBrxUFBZ-3b7sZUqt-8z1WtAAP9E0w/viewform?usp=dialog)


1. Fill out all information, including name, email, phone number, reason for ban

2. Please write a well crafted, thoughtful paragraph on why we should unban you.
