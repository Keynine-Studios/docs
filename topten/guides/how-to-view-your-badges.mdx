<Warning>
  After the release of Top 10! 2.0 and the UI refresh, the video in this article was out of date. The steps should work the same but if you have any troubles, please create a ticket! A new video is being created!
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title='From the "Explore" Tab'>
    1. Click on "Profile" on the bottom bar
    2. Scroll down to the "Badges" section
    3. Wait for the badges to load
    4. Click on one to enlarge it
  </Step>
  <Step title="Completed!">
    You now can view your badges in Top 10!
  </Step>
</Steps>
