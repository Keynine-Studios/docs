<Note>
  To get AI suggestions for a location, that location must be In Progress and not Completed.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title='From the "Explore" tab'>
    1. Click on "My Top 10s" on the bottom bar
    2. Click on the location that you want to get suggestions for
    3. Click on "Get AI Suggestions" at the top of the page
    4. Click on the category you want to get suggestions for
    5. Once the suggestions load, you will get 5 options
    6. If you would like to get directions you can click "Open in Maps"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Completed!">
    You successfully got AI Suggestions in Top 10!
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Once you leave this page, the suggestions are not saved, and clicking on "Get AI Suggestions" again may yield different results as it is re-prompting the AI engine.
</Warning>
