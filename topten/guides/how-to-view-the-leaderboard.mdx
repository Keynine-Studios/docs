<Steps>
  <Step title='From the "Explore" tab'>
    1. Click "Friends" on the bottom bar
    2. Click "Leaderboard"
    3. At the top, you can filter by either "Photos" or "Places"
    4. Places will give you the amount of Completed Top 10s a person has done
    5. Photos will give you the amount of Photos a person has done
  </Step>
  <Step title="Completed!">
    You know can view the leaderboard in Top 10!
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Loading times may vary based on internet connection and where you are in the world as the leaderboard function uses some background APIs
</Info>
