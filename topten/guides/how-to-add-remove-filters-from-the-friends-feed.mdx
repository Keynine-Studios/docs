<Warning>
  After the release of Top 10! 2.0 and the UI refresh, the video in this article may be out of date. The steps should work the same but if you have any troubles, please create a ticket!
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title='From the "Explore" tab'>
    1. Click on "Feed" on the bottom bar
    2. Click on "Filters"
    3. Multi-select the filters you want to add
    4. Click "Apply Filters"
    5. To remove filters, you can click the "X" next to one on the Feed view, or select Clear All
  </Step>
  <Step title="Completed!">
    You now can use filters in Top 10!
  </Step>
</Steps>
