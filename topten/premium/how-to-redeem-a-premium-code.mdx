<Tip>
  If you received a link instead refer to [this article](https://getsupport.keyninestudios.com/topten/premium/how-to-redeem-a-premium-code-via-link)
</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="From the main page">
    1. Tap on "Profile" on the bottom navigation bar
    2. Click on "Upgrade to Pro" button
    3. Scroll down
    4. Click "Redeem Offer Code"
    5. Enter the redeem code you were given
    6. Click "Redeem Code"
    7. Double click the lock button
    8. Verify with Face ID
  </Step>
  <Step title="Completed!">
    You know have premium!
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  If you do not receive pro access immediately that is normal, wait until you get the email from Apple titled "Subscription Confirmed" then check again. After this point, if you still do not have access contact support.
</Info>

<Info>
  If you do not see the Upgrade to Pro button and instead see a ⭐️ next to your name. You are already a premium member and cannot redeem a code!
</Info>
