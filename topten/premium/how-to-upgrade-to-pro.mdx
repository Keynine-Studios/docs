  After the release of Top 10! 2.0 and the UI refresh, the video in this article may be out of date. The steps should work the same but if you have any troubles, please create a ticket!

<Info>
  In this video you see a Xcode notification come up, that is because this is a simulated purchase, when actually purchasing Top 10! Pro, you will be billed 3.99/month and you will not get this notification.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title='From the "Explore" tab'>
    1. Click "Profile"
    2. Click "Upgrade to Pro" button
    3. Scroll down
    4. Click "Upgrade"
    5. Double click the power button on the right side of your iPhone
    6. Verify with FaceId
    7. You are now subscribed
  </Step>
  <Step title="Completed!">
    You now have signed up for Top 10! Pro.
  </Step>
</Steps>
