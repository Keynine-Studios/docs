<Warning>
  If you do not receive pro access immediately that is normal, wait until you get the email from Apple titled "Subscription Confirmed" then check again. After this point, if you still do not have access contact support.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  If you received a code, use [this article instead](https://getsupport.keyninestudios.com/hc/en-us/articles/50232027256211)
</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="From the Explore Tab">
    1. Click on the URL, it should open in the app store
    2. Click "Redeem"
    3. Double click the lock button and verify with your Facial ID
  </Step>
  <Step title="Completed!">
    You now have premium
  </Step>
</Steps>
