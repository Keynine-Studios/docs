## Steps:

**From the "Explore" tab:**

NOTE: If you do not see the **Join Event** button on step three, it may mean there isn't an event going on. Please review [Event Dates](https://getsupport.keyninestudios.com/hc/en-us/articles/50231881295379) for more.


1. Click my "Top 10s" on the bottom bar

2. Click "Events Center"

3. Click "Join Event"

4. To add a photo, click on My Top 10 at the bottom

5. Select the category

6. Follow [these steps](https://getsupport.keyninestudios.com/article/9-how-to-take-upload-an-image) to finish taking the photo as they are the same in both instances

7. To view a public feed of others participating in the event, simply click on "Public Feed"

To go directly into the events section of the app, click [here](http://topten.keyninestudios.com/download)
