<Info>
  If you do not see the **Join Event** button on step three, it may mean there isn't an event going on. Please review [Event Dates](https://getsupport.keyninestudios.com/topten/premium/event-dates) for more.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="To Open an Event">
    1. Click my "Top 10s" on the bottom bar
    2. Click "Events Center"
    3. Click "Join Event"
    4. To add a photo, click on My Top 10 at the bottom
    5. Select the category
    6. Follow [these steps](https://getsupport.keyninestudios.com/topten/guides/how-to-take-upload-a-picture) to finish taking the photo as they are the same in both instances
    7. To view a public feed of others participating in the event, simply click on "Public Feed"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Completed!">
    You now have participated in an event!
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  To go directly into the events section of the app, click [here](http://topten.keyninestudios.com/download)
</Tip>
