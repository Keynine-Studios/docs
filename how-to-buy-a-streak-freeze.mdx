### Frequent Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is a streak freeze?">
    A streak freeze is a digital item that allows you to preserve your streak even when you do not completed you lesson for that day.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are notes?">
    Notes are Cent's form of currency. You can view how many notes you have from the top right corner of the "Skills" page.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How many streak freezes do I have?">
    To view how many streak freezes you currently have, simply follow the steps above to buy a streak freeze, your current inventory will be right underneath the shop page.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
